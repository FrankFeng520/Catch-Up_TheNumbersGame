The Rules

The Catch Up Game is a turn-based game for two players. Here are the rules:

  • The game starts with a selective amount of numbers displayed on the screen, but feel free to add or remove numbers with the options bar on the left
  • Players take turns selecting numbers.
  • The selected number is added to the player's score and removed from the choices. The middle box is a comparison box, it displays the difference of both players' scores
  • You can select multiple numbers on the same turn. Therefore take advantage of the comparison box because it will tell you how many points you can take on that single turn.
  • After choosing a number, if your score matches or exceeds your opponent's, it becomes their turn.
  • The game continues until no numbers remain in the field. The player with the highest score at the end wins.
  • If you encounter a interesting scenario, and want to go back, there is a "undo" button which only permitted if both players agree

Good luck and have fun! 🧡
